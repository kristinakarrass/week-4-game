Rules of the Game

You will be given a random number at the start of the game.

Can you spot the emojis? By clicking on an emoji, you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.

You win the game by matching your total score to the random number, you lose the game if your total scores goes above it.

The value of each crystal is hidden from you until you click it.

Each time the game starts over, the values of the emojis will change.
