Instructions

You will be given a random number at the start of each game.

Can you spot the emojis? By clicking on an emoji, you will add a specific amount of points to your total score. The value of each emoji is hidden from you until you click it and it changes every time you start a new game.

Win the game by matching your total score to the random number, you lose the game if your total score goes above it.
